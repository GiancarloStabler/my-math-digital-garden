#definition #abstract-algebra/group-theory

#### Definition
For group $G$ and subgroup $H$, the normalizer of $P$ in $G$ is the largest subgroup $N\leq G$ such that $H \unlhd N$.

I think.
#### Note
If $H \unlhd G$ is a normal subgroup, then $N_{G}(H)=G$.

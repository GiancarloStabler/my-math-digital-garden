#definition #abstract-algebra/group-theory 

#### Definition
A group homomorphism between two groups $( G, \cdot )$ and $( H, \ast )$ is a map $f:( G, \cdot )\to ( H, \ast )$ such that for elements $x,y\in G$, $f(x \cdot y) = f(x) \ast f(y)$.
